The App that let's you Battle for your workout

Looking for extra motivation in your exersize routine? You'll love RPG-Fit. This app allows users to battle against a
dungeon and dragons'character for workout motivation. Choose your workout ahead of time from a menu of workout choices,
pick your monster to battle and you're ready to get RPG-Fit. Your monster will send challenges... do 20 push-ups
for instance. Complete your challenge if you want to beat the monster.
