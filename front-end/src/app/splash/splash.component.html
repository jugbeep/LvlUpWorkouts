The App that let's you Battle for your Workout

Looking for extra motivation in your exercise routine? You'll love RPG-Fit. This app allows users to battle
against a fantasy monster for workout motivation. Choose your workout ahead of time from a menu of
workout choices, pick your monster to battle and you're ready to get RPG-Fit. Your monster will send a
challenge... do 20 push-ups for instance. Complete your challenge if you want to beat the monster.

Can you beat the monster? Let's get started.
